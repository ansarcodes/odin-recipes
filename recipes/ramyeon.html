Description

Korean instant noodles, Korean ramen or just just ramyeon is a Korean version of Japanese ramen or better to say Japanese instant noodles. In Japan, ramen can be instant or fresh, but in Korea, ramyeon is always instant.

Even though it is treated as a dish, which you can order at eateries, ramyeon refers to instant noodles, usually with some vegetables and eggs.

The most popular one, at least in Korea, is Shin Ramyun(alt. Shin Ramen) - a spicy instant noodles in red package, but there are countless variations of noodles as well as the ways to make them.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Korean ramyeon
  • 550 mL of water (about 2 1/3 cups)
  • 1 egg
  • green onions

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add noodles, soup base, and vegetable mix.
  3. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add an egg and cook for 1-2 minute more.
  5. Remove from the heat and serve with chopped onions on top.
